The Vine is your premier destination for fine wine!

We are an upscale, service oriented wine warehouse with a focus on exploring new wine regions, flavours and types. We carry hundreds of wines that are unique to our store for you to enjoy. Our goal is making quality domestic and international wines more accessible to wine lovers, while at the same time, providing knowledgeable guidance. We take great pride in the quality of our products and services, and ultimately, satisfying our customers at every available opportunity.

What We Do

At The Vine, we aim to provide an unmatched shopping experience by providing wine lovers with the opportunity to:

  • • Purchase quality-focused wines not otherwise retailed in their province
  • • Order wine and wine accessories from the comfort of their home.
  • • Have wine delivered to their door
  • • Research each wine before purchasing it.
  • • Access fine wine in rural regions

About The Wines

We travel regularly to the world's leading wine regions in search of great values and outstanding selections. As well, we recognize the quality of wine being made in "our own backyard". As such, we make regular visits to our local wineries, and make the stand-outs available to our customers. Don't forget to take advantage of 5% off our already great prices on any purchase of 12 bottles or more. We also ship your wine throughout Canada - Spend $200 and we pick up the delivery tab!
